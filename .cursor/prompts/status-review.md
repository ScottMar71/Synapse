# Review initiative or outcome delivery status.

## Recommended Skills

- `product-manager`
- `new-outcome`
- `product-designer`
- `tech-lead`
- `implementation-engineer`
- `delivery-manager`

## Prompt

- Read the relevant outcome overview, linked outcomes, and ticket files.
- Use the `delivery-manager` skill when the user wants status, blockers, or next steps.
- Lead with blockers, risks, and testing gaps.
- Keep the review concise and action-oriented.
